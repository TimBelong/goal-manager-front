Account Deletion for Goals Track

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the Goals Track application.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Navigate to the Settings screen.
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
  5. Tap the Delete Account button.
  6. Confirm the action in the popup dialog by tapping Delete.

Data Deletion Policy

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our servers immediately. We do not retain any information after the deletion is confirmed.

Types of Data Deleted:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • User Content: All created goals, months, tasks, sub-goals, and daily activity logs.

Retention Period: 0 days (Deleted immediately upon request).
